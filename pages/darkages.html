I ❤️ DAoC

 My MMO Obsession

  My MMO obsession began in 2002 when a friend of mine introduced me to "Dark Ages of Camelot". At the time the expansion "Trials of Atlantis" had just been released. Before then I had never seen or played a massively multiplayer online game.
  DAoC is a game based loosely upon three differing mythologies of the Norse, the Celts and Arthurian legend.
  Naturally, being a fantasy MMO game with strong competitive and collaborative elements it ticked all of my boxes and I have played it off and on for over 21 years, along with Final Fantasy 11, Final Fantasy 14, Eve Online and World of Warcraft.
  I am waiting for a similiar fantasy MMO game to be publicly released for VR, at which point you'll probably need a crow bar to separate me from my headset.
