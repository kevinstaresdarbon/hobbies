I ❤️ Virtual Reality

 My VR Obsession

  My VR obsession began a few years ago with the release of the Occulus Rift. I bought one and instantly fell in love with the virtual reality sphere of computing and gaming.
  Recently I upgraded my headset to a Quest 3 from meta and am finding another passion in the vr sphere; Mixed Reality.
  Very recently I have been enjoying productivity applications like "Immersed" to create collaborative rooms and virtual desktop experiences and am deeply enjoying placing VR elements within my front room.
  In gaming terms I am currently playing Demeo and Red Matter. I am very happy with my Quest 3 and can see myself enjoying it for many years to come.
